Oskar Dabrowski

Oskar Dąbrowski is young Polish artist focused on abstract paintings and drawings as well as photography. His creations are inspired by his girlfriend and close environment.

“I am young and I do not intend to specify my style, I do not close myself in one way of painting, creating. I try a lot of different techniques and experiment all the time.
I hope that at some point one might say that I have worked out a specific style, then it will have to have its own name.
